### POST Headers

    Content-Type:application/json
    X-Client-Type:Android
    VERSION-NAME:6.x.x
    CELL-VIP-ID:xxxxxxxx
    SLOT-INDEX:1  (1 OR 2)
    OPERATOR:xxxxx  (MCC+MNC 46000\46001...)
    UUID:xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx (UNIQUE FILE ID 唯一文件ID)
