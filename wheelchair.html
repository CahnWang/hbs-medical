    JBH Medical is a leading manufacturer of wheelchairs and mobility scooters based in China. The company was founded in 2013. We specialize in designing, developing, and producing high-quality assistive devices for people with mobility impairments.

     
    JBH Medical has obtained more than 50 patents, and our products have been exported to over 100 countries in the world, covering Europe, South America, North America, Australia, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and other countries. We are actively seeking global agents and distributors to represent our products, and looking forward to cooperating with you.

    WHEELCHAIR EXPERTS

    We are committed to solving the problem of travel difficulties for the global aging population and limited mobility persons.

    JBH Group, founded in March 2013, is a progessional manufacturer of portable electric wheelchairs and mobility scooters. We are committed to solving the problem of travel difficulties for the global aging population and limited mobility persons. The company focuses on the development and production of portable intelligent electric wheelchairs,  electric folding mobility scooter, sports wheelchairs, intelligent nursing beds and various mobility aids.
